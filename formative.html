Our Aim

We aim to change the way that music can be used in therapy to calm patients and help influence their thought processes.

We use machine learning algorithms which are able to recognise emotions in music and recommend music dependent on the patient's current mental state.

Our database is still young but as we continue to collate more and more songs, our algorithms will become further advanced and be able to recognise a larger range emotions to apply them to a greater number of conditions.

Research

We have worked extensively to understand the psychological benefits of music when undergoing stressful circumstances such as medical treatment or mental illness.

The Harvard Medical School has stated "Music therapy can calm anxiety, ease pain, and provide a pleasant diversion during chemotherapy or a hospital stay." [1] in their research paper exploring music therapy.
